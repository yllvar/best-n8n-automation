### 1. **Create E-mail Responses with Fastmail and OpenAI**

Email replies. Time-consuming, repetitive, soul-crushing. Not anymore.

**What it does:** Automates drafting personalized email replies in Fastmail using OpenAI. Analyzes incoming messages, generates context-aware responses, saves them as drafts for your review.

**The Relief:** AI that understands context and tone. No more "Dear Sir/Madam" to someone you've been emailing for years. Professional replies, personalized properly, ready for sending.

### 2. **Receive Daily Market News from FT.com to Outlook**

Financial news. Important. Also overwhelming. Usually unread because who has time?

**What it does:** Fetches and summarizes daily financial news from FT.com, delivers a structured HTML digest to your Outlook inbox using Google Gemini for summarization.

**Why This Matters:** Market information without drowning in articles. The AI reads everything, you read the summary. It's like having a Bloomberg terminal that respects your time.

### 3. **Summarize Your Emails with AI and Send to Line Messenger**

Too many emails. Not enough time. Need summaries immediately.

**What it does:** Reads emails via IMAP, summarizes them with OpenRouter's Llama model, pushes concise summaries (with action items and urgency detection) to Line Messenger.

**The Mobile Advantage:** Email summaries on your phone without opening your actual inbox. Revolutionary for people who value their mental health.

### 4. **Send Specific PDF Attachments from Gmail to Google Drive Using OpenAI**

Email attachments. Dozens arrive daily. Finding the important ones manually? Nightmare fuel.

**What it does:** Filters Gmail attachments using OpenAI to match keywords (invoices, contracts, reports), uploads relevant PDFs to specified Google Drive folders.

**The Organization:** Automatic filing without the filing. Invoices go to Invoices. Contracts go to Contracts. Magic, basically.
